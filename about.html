About

Welcome to MinimalistPicks. We are a small editorial team writing about the everyday objects that quietly make life better — the linen sheet, the heavy mug, the cable that never frays.

What we believe

Most "best of" lists are noise: dozens of options, padded specs, paid placements dressed as opinions. We believe the opposite — that you only need one good recommendation per category, and that it should be tested in real life, not in a spec sheet.

How we pick

  • We buy products with our own money or borrow them from friends. We do not accept payment for placement.
  • We live with each item for at least two weeks before writing.
  • If we wouldn't recommend it to a close friend, we don't write about it.

How we make money

This site contains affiliate links — when you buy through us, we may earn a small commission, at no extra cost to you. Those commissions keep the lights on so we can keep writing honestly. We also display contextual ads via Google AdSense.
